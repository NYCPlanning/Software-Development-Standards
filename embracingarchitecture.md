## Adapting and Embracing Architecture 

1. Align Architecture with Business Goals. 
2. Emphasize Modularity and Separation of Concerns. 
3. Choose Scalable and Flexible Architecture. 
4. Event-Driven and Data-Centric Design. 
5. Focus on Performance and Non-Functional Requirements. 
6. Data Pipeline Best Practices. 
7. Continuous Integration / Continuous Deployment (CI/CD). 
8. Security and Compliance. 
9. Iterative Growth and Maintainability.
